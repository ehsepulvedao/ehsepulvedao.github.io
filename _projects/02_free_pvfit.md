FREE – PV-FIT explores functional and time-frequency representations for the detection and interpretation of anomalies in photovoltaic signals. The project seeks to bridge signal processing, artificial intelligence and explainable fault diagnosis through physically meaningful indicators and interpretable tools for maintenance decisions.

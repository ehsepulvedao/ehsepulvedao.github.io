SOL-DAQ aims to develop a diagnostic-oriented instrumentation and data acquisition platform for photovoltaic systems. The project focuses on sensor placement, data quality, acquisition strategies, signal processing and fault-oriented monitoring.

The objective is to support the development of reliable and interpretable diagnostic methods for photovoltaic systems operating under real-world conditions.

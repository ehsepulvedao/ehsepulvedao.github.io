SOL-DAQ aims to develop a diagnostic-oriented instrumentation and data acquisition platform for photovoltaic systems. The project focuses on sensor selection, acquisition architecture, data quality, signal conditioning, storage, processing and exploitation of photovoltaic measurements under real operating conditions. The long-term objective is to support robust, interpretable and deployable diagnostic methods for photovoltaic systems and next-generation solar power plants.

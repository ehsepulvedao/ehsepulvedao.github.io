CSPG – ALARMES focuses on the modeling of photovoltaic systems and the automatic extraction of operational alarms from system behavior. The project connects physical modeling, operational data and diagnostic indicators in order to support the supervision of photovoltaic installations within the CSPG research team at PROMES-CNRS.
